# **🏡 Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard**
### *An Interactive Power BI Dashboard for Analyzing Global Airbnb Listings, Market Trends, Pricing, and Customer Ratings*
The Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard is a comprehensive business intelligence solution built in Power BI to analyze Airbnb's global marketplace. It enables users to explore listing growth, host activity, pricing strategies, city-wise market share, room type performance, and customer satisfaction across major cities worldwide. The dashboard supports data-driven decision-making for property owners, investors, tourism analysts, and hospitality businesses.

# **📌 Short Description / Purpose**
The Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard provides an interactive view of Airbnb's worldwide marketplace by tracking key performance indicators such as total listings, hosts, property types, customer reviews, pricing, room types, and city-level market performance.
The dashboard helps stakeholders identify market opportunities, evaluate pricing strategies, compare city performance, and understand customer experience to optimize business growth and maximize occupancy.

# **🛠 Tech Stack**
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Primary platform for creating interactive reports and dashboards.
* 📂 **Power Query** – Used for data extraction, cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Developed calculated measures, KPIs, cumulative percentages, and dynamic analytics.
* 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Built relationships among listings, hosts, locations, room types, and reviews to enable accurate cross-filtering and reporting.
* 📈 **Interactive Visualizations** – KPI Cards, Area Charts, Line Charts, Bar Charts, Pareto Charts, Heatmaps, Tables, and Navigation Buttons.
* 📁 **File Formats**
  * `.pbix` – Dashboard development
  * `.png` – Dashboard preview
  * Excel/CSV – Source dataset

# **📂 Data Source**
**Source:** Maven Analytics
The dataset contains Airbnb listing information collected from multiple cities worldwide, including:
* Listing details
* Host information
* Property type
* Room type
* Review counts
* Price
* City and country
* Superhost status
* Customer ratings
* Location attributes
The data was cleaned, transformed, and modeled to generate meaningful business insights and interactive reporting.

# **✨ Features / Highlights**
## **📍 Business Problem**\
The global short-term rental market is highly competitive, with millions of listings across different cities and property types. Property owners and business analysts often struggle to answer important business questions such as:
* Which cities dominate the Airbnb market?
* How has Airbnb listing growth changed over time?
* Which room types generate premium pricing?
* What percentage of listings belong to Superhosts?
* Which cities provide the highest customer satisfaction?
* Where should investors focus for future opportunities?
Without interactive analytics, these insights require significant manual analysis across multiple datasets.

# **🎯 Goal of the Dashboard**
The dashboard is designed to:
* Monitor Airbnb marketplace performance worldwide.
* Analyze listing growth trends over time.
* Compare city-wise market share.
* Evaluate pricing across different room types.
* Assess customer ratings across multiple service dimensions.
* Support investment, pricing, and operational decisions through interactive visual analytics.

# **📊 Walkthrough of Key Visuals**
## **1. KPI Summary Cards**
The dashboard opens with key marketplace metrics that provide an overview of Airbnb's global ecosystem.
Key KPIs include:
* **Total Listings:** 279,712
* **Total Hosts:** 182,024
* **Property Types:** 144
* **Customer Reviews:** 5.373 Million
These KPIs allow stakeholders to quickly understand the scale and performance of the Airbnb platform.

## **2. New Listings Trend (Area & Line Chart)**
This visualization tracks Airbnb listing growth over multiple years while comparing room types, including
* Entire Place
* Private Room
* Shared Room
* Hotel Room
The chart illustrates market expansion, seasonal trends, and changes in accommodation preferences over time.

## **3. Market Share by City (Pareto Chart)**
The Pareto analysis ranks cities based on the number of Airbnb listings while distinguishing between:
* Superhost Listings
* Non-Superhost Listings
* Cumulative Market Share (%)
This helps identify cities that contribute the largest share of Airbnb inventory and highlights where Superhost presence is strongest.

## **4. Average Price by Room Type (Horizontal Bar Chart)**
This chart compares the average listing price across different accommodation categories:
* Hotel Room
* Entire Place
* Shared Room
* Private Room
It enables users to understand pricing differences and identify premium accommodation segments within the Airbnb marketplace.

## **5. Customer Ratings Heatmap**
The ratings table compares customer satisfaction across major cities using multiple evaluation criteria, including:
* Accuracy
* Communication
* Cleanliness
* Location
* Value
Conditional formatting highlights high-performing and lower-performing cities, making it easy to identify areas of service excellence and opportunities for improvement.

## **6. Navigation Buttons**
Interactive navigation buttons enable users to seamlessly switch between dashboard pages, allowing focused analysis of:
* New Listings
* Market Share by City
* Customer Ratings
This enhances usability and improves the overall analytical experience.

# **📈 Business Impact & Insights**
### **Market Expansion Analysis**
The dashboard helps Airbnb analysts identify cities with the highest listing concentration, supporting strategic market expansion and competitive analysis.

### **Investment Decision Support**
Real estate investors can compare market size, pricing trends, and customer satisfaction across cities to identify attractive investment opportunities.

### **Pricing Optimization**
Average room pricing analysis enables hosts to benchmark their listings against market averages and develop competitive pricing strategies.

### **Host Performance Evaluation**
Superhost analysis provides insights into host quality and service standards, helping Airbnb understand the distribution of experienced hosts across cities.

### **Customer Experience Improvement**
The ratings dashboard highlights strengths and weaknesses across service dimensions, enabling hosts and property managers to improve guest satisfaction and overall review scores.

### **Data-Driven Business Decisions**
By combining marketplace growth, pricing, market share, and customer feedback into a single interactive dashboard, stakeholders can make informed decisions that improve operational performance and maximize revenue.

# **🔍 Key Insights**
* The Airbnb marketplace includes **279,712 active listings** managed by **182,024 hosts**, offering **144 unique property types** and generating over **5.37 million customer reviews**.
* **Entire Place** listings dominate the platform, while **Hotel Rooms** command the highest average nightly prices, reflecting their premium positioning.
* A small number of cities contribute a significant share of total listings, as illustrated by the Pareto analysis, helping identify the most influential markets.
* **Superhost** listings represent a key segment of the marketplace, providing insight into host quality and customer trust across major cities.
* Customer ratings across **accuracy, communication, cleanliness, location, and value** reveal consistently high service quality, while also highlighting cities with opportunities for operational improvement.
* The dashboard equips hosts, investors, and business analysts with actionable insights to optimize pricing strategies, enhance guest experiences, identify high-potential markets, and support data-driven decision-making in the global short-term rental industry.
